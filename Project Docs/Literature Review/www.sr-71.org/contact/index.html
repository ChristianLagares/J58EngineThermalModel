If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions, feel free to write to me. However, please try to avoid asking questions that may be answered by this web site or on the FAQs page. You may also find the SR-71 Online Message Boards a great place to find answers to research questions.

All electronic mail concerning this site should be directed to Paul Kucher using the form below. If you have any electronic contributions, please initiate the request using the form and you will hear back from me. The reason why this form exists is to counteract spam.

Please submit all technical problems and errors, as well as dead links. These submissions help make this site better for future visitors, and your help is greatly appreciated in bringing this site to its highest potential. Finally, please keep your message pithy. It will help me better answer your questions if you stay brief and interesting.

I now sign all messages with GNU Privacy Guard so you can trust they are from me. Please download my public key if this feature interests you.

You may also send snail mail to the following address:

Paul Kucher
