Paul Kucher is an Electrical Engineering graduate student at Michigan State University. His areas of interest include mixed-signal and analog VLSI design, particularly the applications of adaptive technologies. His personal homepage is at www.kucher.org.

He has had an interest in aviation since 1994 and has been researching the Blackbird and other related topics since 1998. He founded this web site to promote the history of the Blackbird and general military aviation afterwards. In addition, it is his goal to visit every surviving Blackbird. Currently, he has seen 28 of 30 (SR-71A #61-7962 and SR-71A #61-7979 still remaining) Blackbirds at least once. Below are some pictures of Paul with the Blackbirds.
