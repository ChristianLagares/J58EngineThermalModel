March Field Air Museum

Home » Museums » March Field Museum

The March Field Air Museum is located near Riverside, CA just outside of March Joint Air Reserve Base. The museum is home to over 70 aircraft, including favorites such as the SR-71, B-17, B-29, and B-52. The museum is also home to one of the two YA-9A prototypes, an experimental attack aircraft that competed in the A-X program in the early 1970s. This guide covers the aircraft that were at the museum during summer of 2009.
