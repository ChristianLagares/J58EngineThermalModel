Presidential Aircraft Hangar

Home » Museums » NMUSAF » Presidential Aircraft Hangar

The Presidential Aircraft Hangar is a part of the museum's annex located at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. It is home to several famous aircraft that include VC-137C #26000 and VC-121E Columbine III, as well as a few non-presidential airplanes.
