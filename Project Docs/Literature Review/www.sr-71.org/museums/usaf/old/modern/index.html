Modern Flight Hangar

Home » Museums » NMUSAF » Old » Modern Flight Hangar

The Modern Flight Hangar covers aviation from the 1950s to the present. It is the largest hangar at the USAF Museum and houses huge aircraft, such as the B-52D, C-124C, and XB-70A. There is something of interest in all areas of the Modern Flight Hangar; it includes the B-58A, SR-71A, TACIT BLUE, X-15, X-29A, XB-70A, YF-117A, and YF-22 to name a few.
