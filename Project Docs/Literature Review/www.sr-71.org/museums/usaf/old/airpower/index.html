Air Power/Korean Conflict Hangar

Home » Museums » NMUSAF » Old » Air Power/Korean Conflict Hangar

The Air Power/Korean Conflict Hangar covers World War II through the Korean Conflict in the mid-1950s. There are also a few early Vietnam Conflict-era aircraft on display. The hangar is home to over 60 aircraft and missiles. This site covers nearly all of the aircraft on display in this hangar as well as other areas of interest such as the XB-36 Tire.
