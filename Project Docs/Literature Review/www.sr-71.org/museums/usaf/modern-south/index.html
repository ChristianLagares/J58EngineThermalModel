South Modern Flight Hangar

Home » Museums » NMUSAF » South Modern Flight Hangar

The South Modern Flight Hangar primarily covers aircraft from the Korean Conflict. There are over 15 aircraft on display, including the A-26C, F-84E, F-86A, and F-94A. It is part of the second largest hangar at the museum.
