Castle Air Museum

Home » Museums » Castle Air Museum

The Castle Air Museum is located in Atwater, CA near the former Castle AFB. This museum is home to a number of rare aircraft, such as the SR-71A, B-18, WB-50D, RB-36H, and the Royal Air Force's Avro Vulcan B.2. The aircraft on display here cover a wide range of aviation history, from the 1930s to the present. As the museum continues to grow, it expects to add more modern aircraft like the F-14 that was recently put on display. This guide covers the 49 aircraft that were at the museum during summer of 2006.
