The National Air and Space Museum's Steven F. Udvar-Hazy Center is located in Chantilly, VA on the grounds of the Washington Dulles International Airport. Before 2003, the Paul E. Garber Preservation, Restoration, and Storage Facility in Suitland, MD held the majority of the NASM's collection due to the inadequate size of the downtown museum. These photos are from opening day, 15 December 2003. More photos of SR-71A #61-7972 on display at the Udvar-Hazy Center are available in the Blackbird Image Gallery.
