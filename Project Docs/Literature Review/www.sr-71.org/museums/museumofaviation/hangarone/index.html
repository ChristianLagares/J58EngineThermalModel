Hangar One

Home » Museums » Museum of Aviation » Hangar One

Hangar One displays restored aircraft, missiles, and engines, as well as an F-105 cockpit simulator. Here, you will find both modern and historic airplanes, from the BT-13A Valiant to the F-4C Phantom II. This building also features several exhibits on some historically important aviators.
