SR-71A #61-7961 is on display at the Kansas Cosmosphere and Space Center in Hutchinson, KS. Assembly of this aircraft started on 13 January 1965 and it rolled out on 19 October 1965. The first and last flights of #961 are on 13 April 1966 and 2 February 1977, respectively. During this time, SR-71A #961 logged a total of 1,601 hours.
