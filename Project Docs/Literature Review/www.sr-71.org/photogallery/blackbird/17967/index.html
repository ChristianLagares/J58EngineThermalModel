SR-71A #61-7967 is on display at the 8th Air Force Museum at Barksdale AFB, LA. SR-71A #61-7967 was one of the two aircraft reactivated in 1995 for Air Force service before the program was cancelled in 1998. Assembly of this aircraft started on 14 July 1965 and its roll out was on 18 April 1966. The first flight of the aircraft was on 3 August 1966. This SR-71A has 2765.5 hrs of flight time. Photos from SR-71 Online Archives were taken on 14 July 2000.
