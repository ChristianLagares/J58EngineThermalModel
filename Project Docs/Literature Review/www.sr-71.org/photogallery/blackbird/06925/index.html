A-12 #06925 is on display at the U.S.S. Intrepid Museum in New York, NY. This aircraft lacks any kind of identification that signifies it as A-12 #60-6925. However, many A-12s during active service did not carry any markings or false markings, making this a historically accurate depiction. As the least-flown A-12 still in existence, this aircraft only logged 177.9 hours. The airplane remains in decent shape considering the harsh display conditions. As the only Blackbird on display aboard an aircraft carrier, it is inappropriately displayed with other carrier-based aircraft, leading to mass confusion by casual Blackbird fans. These photos were taken on 12 June 2000 and 28 July 2002.
