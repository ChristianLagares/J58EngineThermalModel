YF-12A #06935 is on display at the USAF Museum near Dayton, OH. It is the only YF-12A in the world and resides in the R&D Hangar of the National Museum of the United States Air Force Annex. The YF-12A was used by NASA from 1969 through 1979 before its last flight on 7 November 1979, when it became the first permanent display of any Blackbird at the time.
